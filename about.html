Our Story

Cafe Verde was born from a simple belief: that great food and environmental responsibility should go hand in hand. Founded in the heart of Zelienople, Pennsylvania, we've made it our mission to serve fresh, locally-sourced meals that nourish both our community and our planet.

Every dish we create tells a story of passion, flavor and sustainability, from the farms where our ingredients are grown to the plates they're served on. We believe that when you choose fresh, local, and sustainable, you're not just making a meal choice – you're making a choice for the future.

Our commitment extends beyond our kitchen. We work closely with local farmers, support sustainable agriculture practices, and strive to minimize our own environmental impact, as well as educate others.
